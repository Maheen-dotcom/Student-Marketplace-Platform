Welcome to Student Marketplace

A dedicated platform where students can buy and sell books, laptops, calculators, notes and other educational resources.

📚 Books

Buy and sell textbooks, reference books and study guides at affordable prices.

💻 Laptops

Find second-hand laptops and accessories suitable for academic use.

🧮 Calculators

Exchange scientific and engineering calculators among students.

📝 Notes

Share and purchase quality academic notes, assignments and learning material.

Why Use Student Marketplace?

  • Easy Product Management
  • Fast Product Search
  • Student-Friendly Platform
  • Affordable Academic Resources
  • Simple and Secure User Management

Project Features

This platform provides user registration, login, product management, search functionality, category filtering and dashboard analytics through a FastAPI backend connected to a PostgreSQL database.
